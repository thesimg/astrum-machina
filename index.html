Astrum Machina
A Apparatus Ad Astra

Astrum Machina is a generative art project, built with p5.js and automated using Python and Selenium. I created Astrum out of the urge to create something that keeps on creating.

The bot posts every 4 hours on Twitter, and I manually post the best pieces on Instagram. I have, to this point, been unable to get an Instagram bot working without being shadowbanned immediately, because Instagram does not provide a public bot API.
